  • Who is Hayao Miyazaki?

Hayao Miyazaki, an animation director from Japan, is known for co-founding the famous Studio Ghibli. He has been the main director and storyteller for Ghibli films such as Spirited Away, My Neighbor Totoro, Ponyo, and Howl's Moving Castle.
To me and many others, the energy that he creates in his movies is unique. To watch his movies and hear the music is to dream. Miyazaki's movies are top of the line when it comes to capturing magic in possibilities. Miyazaki's main elements in his films involve family, nature, love, and more. His films perfectly take human emotions and animate them.
